How to Play

  • Click a card in your hand to ask the computer for that rank.
  • If the computer has any, you get all of them and can ask again.
  • If not, you'll have to "Go Fish" by clicking the deck to draw a card.
  • If you get the card you asked for, you go again. If not, it's the computer's turn.
  • If you collect all four cards of a rank, you lay down a "book".
  • The game ends when all books are made. Whoever has the most books wins.
